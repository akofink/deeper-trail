# QUICK PITCHES (choose one to anchor early writing)

## Pitch A — “The Signal”
Early game road signs contain a repeating glyph. Later, that glyph is a star map coordinate system. The deeper you travel, the more the *map itself* changes scale.

## Pitch B — “The Persistent Object”
Everything changes between layers except your vehicle. Why? The upgrades are not just mechanical; they’re keys to traversal between realities.

## Pitch C — “The Fabrication Language”
You find fragments of a “build language.” At first it’s recipes. Then it’s instructions for how places assemble themselves. By endgame, crafting is how you alter the world graph.
